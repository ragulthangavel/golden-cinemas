ABOUT US

Golden Cinema was promoted by AP International Group and Ayngaran International, UK. AP International Group, specialized in Film financing and is a Leading Distributor in South India. Have distributed more than 700 movies and has a World-wide distribution network. Ayngaran International from UK, who have been leading distributors of Tamil films in the UK and Europe headquartered at Wimbledon, London

Golden Cinema Theater took over the erstwhile Plaza Cinema in Bur Dubai is the oldest single screen cinema in UAE, was refurbished with new digital projectors and 5.1 DTS sound system. It had been running successfully screening South Indian movies. Golden Cinema Theater was on a lease since 2011 till 2 May 2015. It had leased out the property from AL WASL and the lease was not renewed as the owners have decided to put up a Hilton Hotel at the premise Golden cinema is the leading South Indian Movie distribution company throughout the Gulf,

Conversion

We offer advanced format conversion from DPX, TIFF, TGA and Image based formats to HD/2K/4K Apple Prores, XDCAM, H.264 and MPEG among others. Broadcast standard conversions with 8 Track Dolby E audio.

Restoration

Driven by the goal to revive compromised vintage films and also repair defects in newer films, we offer both automatic and manual restoration services in 4K resolution. In doing this, we ensure that along with preventing the deterioration of your video assets, we also maintain their intrinsic production quality.

Scanning

In this ‘digital’ era, film negatives are gradually becoming obsolete, due to the convenience of Digital Cinema. Golden Cinema is one of the key players in the market to offer the latest and most advanced film scanning services to digitise your 35mm negative and positive film into DPX Files with the highest ‘4K’ resolution.

Subtitling

To ensure that your content reaches a global audience, we offer quality translation and subtitling services, for both your theatrical and post-release requirements. Our team of experts, with their fluency in the source and target languages, strive to capture the essence of the original content with ultimate precision.

Archiving

In order to protect your assets from theft, damage or quality deterioration, we offer conversion of your digital betacam tapes to digital video files for storage and future retrieval.
